Course Details

KSHITIZ

One Year Classroom programme for Class XII students.
Course Name KSHITIZ
Eligibility Class XII appearing students
Who Should Join All students who aspire to crack IIT JEE(Main and Advanced), olympiads and excel in Board exams.
Course Commencement MARCH-MAY
Course Duration 42 weeks

ABOUT THE COURSE

In this course the student gets entire exposure to the syllabus of Class-XI-XII (CBSE, JEE) . Once the complete syllabus is completed revision session of is conducted.

Course Structure

The complete syllabus of Class XI-XII is covered in 2 phases
PHASE SCOPE
PHASE 1(March-May to January) Covering entire Class XI-XII syllabus.
PHASE 2(Revision - February - March) Revision and Doubt clearing session for XI-XII exams with AITS.
